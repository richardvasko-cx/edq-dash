**User:** Ask about: "Braze"

**Gemini:** **Braze** is a customer engagement platform that enables brands to ingest real-time user data, build dynamic audience segments, and deliver highly personalized, multi-channel messages—including email, push notifications, SMS, and in-app messages. 

Conceptually, the platform operates on a continuous cycle of listening to user behaviors, understanding their preferences through segmentation, and acting by triggering targeted campaigns or multi-step journeys (Canvas Flow). 

From a deliverability perspective, Braze orchestrates your outbound email sends, automatically manages suppression lists (for hard bounces, spam complaints, and unsubscribes), and integrates with your DNS configuration to support essential authentication protocols like SPF, DKIM, and DMARC. This infrastructure ensures your highly targeted messages successfully reach the inbox.
