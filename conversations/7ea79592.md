**User:** How do I access Braze

**Gemini:** To access Braze, log into your organization's specific dashboard URL (such as US-01, US-02, or EU-01) using the credentials provided by your team. 

If you do not have an account, an administrator within your organization must invite you. They can add your profile by navigating to the user management settings in their Braze dashboard. 

To learn the platform's core concepts before logging in, you can access the free [Braze Foundations for Everyone](https://learning.braze.com/page/braze-foundations-for-everyone) course, which does not require a Braze account. Technical users can also review the [Getting Started for Developers](https://www.braze.com/docs/developer_guide/getting_started/platform_overview/) guide.

**Next Step:** Contact your company's Braze administrator to request an account invitation to your organization's specific Braze instance.
