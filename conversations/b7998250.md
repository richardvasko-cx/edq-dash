**User:** How can I improve my email deliverability?

**Gemini:** To improve email deliverability, focus on maintaining a clean and engaged recipient list, ensuring proper authentication, and monitoring sending reputation.

Here are key areas to address:

*   **List Hygiene:** Regularly clean your email lists to remove inactive or invalid addresses. Sending to a highly engaged audience signals good sender practices to mailbox providers.
*   **Authentication:** Ensure your SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records are correctly configured. These protocols help verify your sending identity and prevent spoofing.
*   **Sender Reputation:** Monitor your sender reputation closely. High bounce rates or spam complaints can negatively impact your ability to reach the inbox.
*   **Engagement:** Focus on sending relevant content to users who have opted in to receive your emails. High open and click-through rates indicate engaged recipients.
*   **Bounce Management:** Implement a strategy to handle bounces promptly. Hard bounces should be removed immediately, and soft bounces should be monitored and addressed.
*   **IP Warming:** If you are using a new IP address or re-engaging a dormant list, follow a structured IP warming plan to gradually build sending volume and establish a positive reputation. The [IP Warming Planner](https://docs.braze.com/tools/ip_warming_planner/) tool can assist in creating a schedule.
