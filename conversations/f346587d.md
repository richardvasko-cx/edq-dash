**User:** Get Started

**Gemini:** ### Summary
This article introduces Braze as a highly flexible customer engagement platform designed to listen to customer behaviors, understand their signals, and act by sending personalized multi-channel messages. It serves as a foundational guide to help new users grasp core platform concepts, terms, and user segmentation.

### Key Best Practices
* **Complete Foundational Training:** Take the free *Braze Foundations for Everyone* course to quickly learn platform basics.
* **Collaborate Early:** Add colleagues to your Braze workspace immediately to explore and align on platform capabilities.
* **Review Developer Resources:** If handling technical implementation, consult the *Getting Started for Developers* guide.

### Common Pitfalls
* **Over-Standardizing Setup:** Avoid treating default configurations as rigid rules; customize Braze features to fit your organization's unique business goals rather than following a one-size-fits-all approach.

**Next Step:** Access the [Braze Foundations for Everyone](https://learning.braze.com/page/braze-foundations-for-everyone) course to begin your team's onboarding.
