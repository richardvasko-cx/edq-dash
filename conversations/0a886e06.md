**User:** Ask about: "Braze"

**Gemini:** **Braze** is a comprehensive customer engagement platform designed to help brands manage personalized, multi-channel communication with their users. It allows you to ingest real-time customer data, build dynamic segments based on behavior or attributes, and orchestrate targeted campaigns across email, mobile, web, and other channels.

In the context of the active User Guide article, it is introduced as the central hub for your engagement strategy—providing the tools to "listen" to customer behavior and "act" by delivering the right message at the right time.

Key conceptual pillars of the platform include:
*   **Users and Segments:** The foundation of your audience, where you define who receives your messages based on their interactions.
*   **Data Distribution (Currents):** The system for exporting engagement data (like the message engagement or subscription events referenced in your guide excerpts) to external data warehouses or analytics partners.
*   **Campaign Orchestration:** The ability to execute complex, multi-step messaging journeys (such as Canvas) that adapt to user actions, such as app uninstalls or subscription status changes.

If you are troubleshooting deliverability, the platform provides the infrastructure to monitor these engagement signals, which are critical for maintaining a healthy sender reputation and ensuring high inbox placement.
