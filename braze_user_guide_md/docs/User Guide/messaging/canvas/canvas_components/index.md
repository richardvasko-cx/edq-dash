## About Canvas components

With Canvas components, you can unlock new user journeys to improve your process and increase the effectiveness of your audience outreach.

### Customizing user journeys

![Example of a Canvas user journey with a Decision Split step followed by Delay steps and Message steps.](https://www.braze.com/docs/assets/img/canvas_intro/canvas_intro.gif?829819298ce5909b8959e630956375cf){: style="float:right;max-width:55%;margin-left:15px;"}

Use [Action Paths](https://www.braze.com/docs/user_guide/messaging/canvas/canvas_components/action_paths) to split your user journey based on actions and engagement events such as making a purchase. If you want to filter through and target your audiences, [Audience Paths](https://www.braze.com/docs/user_guide/messaging/canvas/canvas_components/audience_paths) help simplify your user targeting by sending your users down different Canvas paths based on audience criteria.

[Decision Split](https://www.braze.com/docs/user_guide/messaging/canvas/canvas_components/decision_split) components use a simple "yes or no" logic to create two mutually exclusive paths for your user journeys that are based on an action or a user attribute. This can help identify and target your user groups.

[Delay](https://www.braze.com/docs/user_guide/messaging/canvas/canvas_components/delay_step) components allow you to delay a single step in your Canvas. This stand-alone delay step in your Canvas is best used for communicating messages to your users at a specific time. Additionally, Delay components may also increase your audience outreach by allowing more time for your audience to meet the component's criteria.

### Testing

When creating your user journeys, you may want to also test for the most effective Canvas path. With [Experiment Paths](https://www.braze.com/docs/user_guide/messaging/canvas/canvas_components/experiment_step), you can test multiple Canvas paths at any step. You can also use the connections between steps as a high-level preview. Orange connections indicate the prior step will immediately advance users to the next step.

### Integration

Want to sync up with your brand's first-party user data? Leverage the available audience sync options for [Facebook](https://www.braze.com/docs/partners/canvas_audience_sync/facebook_audience_sync/) and [Google](https://www.braze.com/docs/partners/canvas_audience_sync/google_audience_sync/).
